Privacy Policy for Language Tutor

Last updated: August 29, 2025

What this extension does

Language Tutor translates text in your browser automtically and provides an easy way to extend your vocabulary.

Data we process

  • Every nth word on screen as setup in settings
  • Language settings (e.g., source and target language).
  • Technical metadata (e.g., IP address, user-agent) that your browser sends to the translation API when making a request.

Purpose

We use this data only to request a translation and show the result to you.

Where the data goes

To provide translations, the selected text is sent to:

  • LibreTranslate (open-source translation service)

Storage & retention

  • We do not store your queries or translations on our servers.
  • No persistent logs are kept by the extension.
  • Any temporary caching occurs locally in your browser and is cleared by normal browsing data cleanup.

Security

Requests are sent over HTTPS.

Your choices

  • You can disable remote translation lookups in the extension’s options.
  • You can uninstall the extension at any time to stop all processing.

Children’s privacy

This extension is not directed to children under 13.
